FAQS

The CVEC is a compulsory contribution which aims to reinforce social, health, cultural and sports support for students as well as to reinforce prevention and health education actions carried out for them.
This contribution is due each academic year by all students enrolled in higher education.

The CVEC must be paid before registering in the school. The code received on the personal certificate after paying will be used to register in the school.

The amount of the CVEC is 92€ for the academic year 2022/2023.

You must log in to the site: cvec.etudiant.gouv.fr and follow the instructions.The payment can be done by credit or debit card.

Once the payment is made, you will receive a certificate of payment with a code (under "Certificate number"). You will then be able to download the document on the schools' online administrative registration and enter your CVEC code.

Go to cvec.etudiant.gouv.fr and click on get my certificate.

Ten things you need to know about CVEC

1

Admission to a French institution of higher education determines whether one is affected by the CVEC.

2

Certain exemptions from the CVEC are recognized

3

The CVEC may be paid online or in cash

4

Even if you are exempt from the CVEC, you must register at cvec.etudiant.gouv.fr

5

The foregoing steps must be completed before you attempt to register at your university or other institution

6

The amount of the CVEC is €95

7

The CVEC is assessed annually

8

In some cases, it is possible to apply for a refund of the CVEC

9

The CVEC improves student life

10

Informations for registration and payment are be available in English
