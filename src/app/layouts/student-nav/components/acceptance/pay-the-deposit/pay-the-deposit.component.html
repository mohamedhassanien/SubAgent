What is the enrollment letter ?

A university issues an enrollment letterÂ to confirm a student's final enrollment at the university. To receive the Letter of Enrollment (LoE), students must pay an initial tuition fee. This amount is required to secure a seat in a specific course at the university.

What is the deposit and how to pay?

A deposit is a partial payment of the total amount due to the School Amount. The deposit is required by all schools after receiving an acceptance the amount varies by school. is generally between 2000 and 5000 euros.

Why paying deposit

Prevent students from booking seats when they plan to change schools.

Manage the number of students admitted, This also allows them to respond quickly to students on the waiting list.

After receiving the deposit, the school will send you the final letter of acceptance to start the VISA procedures.

When to pay it?

In general, the deposit must be paid 3 weeks after receiving admission to the school. After this date, your place is no longer guaranteed.

Can I get a refund?

Before paying the deposit it is always important to read the school's general conditions. However, here are the conditions that we find for the Grandes Ecoles:

In the event of serious unforeseen circumstances

(death, accident, or serious illness), schools may review the situation and grant a refund.

Applicant's Right to Cancel

Applicants may request a refund of the tuition deposit within the 14-day cooling-off period without giving a reason.

The student has not passed the year

If the student has not passed the year or failed to provide a language test and therefore does not have the required admission criteria to pursue his studies.

In case of visa refusal

In case of visa refusal, the school will accept the refund. Of course, you will have to show proof of the refusal.
