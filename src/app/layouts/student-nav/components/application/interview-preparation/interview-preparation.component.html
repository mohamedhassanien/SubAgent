The "D” day

Tips during interview

  • Choose an appropriate outfit, Business attire is required.
  • Log in 5 min prior to the interview. Sometimes, the interview can be delayed. Keep your calm, don’t panic. The jury will directly contact you.
  • Maintain the eye contact. Avoid to fix your notes. Be spontaneous! The interview will be more comfortable for you and the jury.
  • Show your motivation and smile, remember that the jury is most of the time composed of professors that will teach you the course, you want them to want you in their class.
  • Finally, make sure you hang up properly right after the interview.

Examples of Interview questions

    • Introduce yourself and provide a brief overview of your background, experiences, and academic interests.
    • What factors motivated you to choose France as your study destination?
    • What specific aspects of this school/program intrigue you and align with your educational and career goals?
    • Could you share your long-term career aspirations and how you envision yourself in 3 to 5 years?
    • Discuss your strengths and weaknesses, focusing on how they have shaped your personal and academic development.
    • Do you thrive more when working in a team or independently, and why?
    • What is your passion or area of keen interest, and how has it influenced your academic or extracurricular pursuits?
    • Reflect on a past failure or setback you experienced and explain the valuable lesson you learned from it.
    • Share the title and key takeaways from the last book you read, highlighting its impact on your personal growth or perspective.
    • Describe a challenging experience you faced and elaborate on the strategies you employed to navigate through it successfully.
    • Can you provide your thoughts on a current news topic or issue that has caught your attention?
    • In your opinion, what qualities should an effective and successful manager possess?
    • Identify and discuss three significant accomplishments from your academic, professional, or personal life.
    • Share your hobbies and interests outside of academia and explain how they contribute to your personal well-being and development.
    • Who is your role model or someone you admire, and what attributes or achievements of theirs inspire you?
    • Outline your expectations from the MSc program, including specific skills, knowledge, or opportunities you hope to gain.
    • Do you have any inquiries or questions for the interview panel regarding the program, university, or any other relevant topic?

    • Marketing
      • Can you explain the concept of the marketing mix (4Ps) and provide an example of how it can be applied in a real-world marketing campaign?
      • How would you approach market segmentation to target different customer groups effectively?
      • Can you discuss the importance of branding in marketing and provide an example of a successful brand strategy?
      • How would you use social media platforms as marketing tools to engage with and reach a target audience?
      • Can you explain the concept of customer lifetime value (CLV) and its significance in marketing decision-making?
    • Finance
      • Can you explain the concept of compound interest and how it affects the growth of investments over time?
      • How would you evaluate a company's financial health by analyzing its balance sheet and income statement?
      • Can you discuss the role of diversification in investment portfolios and its benefits in managing risk?
      • How would you calculate the return on investment (ROI) for a specific project or investment opportunity?
      • Can you explain the concept of working capital and its importance in assessing a company's short-term liquidity?
    • Supply Chain
      • Can you explain the difference between push and pull supply chain strategies? Provide examples of industries where each strategy may be more suitable.
      • How would you optimize inventory levels to balance customer demand and minimize holding costs?
      • Can you discuss the role of forecasting in supply chain management and provide examples of forecasting methods used in practice?
      • How would you identify and mitigate supply chain risks, such as supplier disruptions or transportation delays?
      • Can you explain the concept of lean manufacturing and its principles for improving operational efficiency?
    • Computer Science
      • Can you explain the difference between a compiler and an interpreter in programming languages?
      • How would you implement a sorting algorithm such as bubble sort or insertion sort in a programming language of your choice?
      • Can you discuss the concept of database normalization and its benefits in reducing data redundancy?
      • How would you approach troubleshooting a computer network connectivity issue?
      • Can you explain the difference between a local variable and a global variable in programming?
    • International Business
      • Can you discuss the impact of cultural differences on international business negotiations and provide strategies to overcome communication barriers?
      • How would you assess the potential risks and benefits of entering a foreign market for a company? Consider factors such as political stability, economic conditions, and legal frameworks.
      • Can you explain the concept of foreign exchange risk and discuss strategies to manage it in international business operations?
      • How would you analyze the competitive landscape of a foreign market and identify potential competitors?
      • Can you discuss the role of multinational corporations in promoting economic growth and development in host countries?
