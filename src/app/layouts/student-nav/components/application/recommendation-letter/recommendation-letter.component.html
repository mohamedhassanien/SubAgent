Recommendation letter

A recommendation letter is a written document that provides insight into the character, abilities, and accomplishments of a student, usually written by a teacher, or other individual who can speak to the student's academic performance and personal qualities.

This letter is typically submitted as part of the student's application package and is used by the university admissions committee to assess the student's suitability for admission.

Tips for the letter

Letters can be written by a teacher or an internship leader/manager that you have or have had in the past.

For bachelor’s degree programs, only one letter is required. For Master’s programs, two letters are required.

Make sure the person’s contact information is included in the letter (email address, phone number).
