What is the housing certificate?

A housing certificate is mandatory when applying for a visa so that the concerned entity guarantees that you have a place to live during your stay in the country.

This certificate can be several things

Accommodation certificate (Attestation d’accueil)
signed by you the person hosting you, and the city
council.

Accommodation certificate (Attestation
d’hebergement) if a family member is hosting you.

Tenancy agreement.

A hotel reservation
