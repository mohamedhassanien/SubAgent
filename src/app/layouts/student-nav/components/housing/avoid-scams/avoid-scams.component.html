How They Get Your Money

Once you contact the scammer, they will do a number of things to try and get your money. Here are a few:

"I am out of the Country, but I'll Send You Keys if You Send Me Money!"

"Act quickly! This amazing apartment at an unbelievably low price won't be available for long."

"We don't have time for a physical viewing, but I can assure you that the apartment is exactly as described in the pictures."

"I apologize for the inconvenience, but I need you to transfer the funds immediately. There are other interested tenants."

"Don't worry about signing a formal contract. We have a verbal agreement, and that's sufficient."
