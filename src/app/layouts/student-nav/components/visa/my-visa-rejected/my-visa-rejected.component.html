What Should I Do ?

If your VISA got refused or you haven’t received an answer which means it got refused implicitly you may contest the refusal within two months, either by filing an appeal with the government or with the Commission de recours contre les décisions de refus de visa d’entrée en France.

You can dispute the decision through the different forms of appeal

RECOURS GRACIEUX

  • RECOURS GRACIEUXA letter must be sent to the consular service, where the visa application was filed,in which you explain in detail the reasons for your visa application.

  • The applicant must also attach either a copy of the letter rejecting the visa applicationor a copy of the application or any other proof of submission of the application inthe case of an implicit refusal.

RECOURS DEVANT LA COMMISSION DE RECOURS

  • The appeal must be sent by registered letter with acknowledgment of receipt presenting your arguments and all useful documents to the following address

Commission de recours contre les refus de visas BP8360944036 NANTES CEDEX 1.

RECOURS EN ANNULATION

  • The last solution is to send an appeal for annulment to the Administrative Court of Nantes, if the two first appeals failed.

CONCLUSION :
If the appeals do not result in the issuance of the visa, the foreigner will have no choice but to file a new visa application.

It is possible to make both an informal appeal and an appeal to the Commissionat the same time.
