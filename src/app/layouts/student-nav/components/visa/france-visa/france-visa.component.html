France visa

Now that you have completed the Campus France (Study in France) procedure and obtained your 'Accord Préalable/Campus France letter', you must now proceed with the visa application on France Visa and ask for a long stay Visa ((VLS-TS):

https://france-visas.gouv.fr/web/france-visas/

Check the video below to complete it

WHAT DOES THE VLS-TS “STUDENT” PERMIT?

Travel freely in all countries of the Schengen area.

Work 964 hours a year, or 20 hours a week, to supplement their income.

Receive a housing allowance from the CAF (check accomodation section)

Extend your stay beyond the validity of the residence permit issued.
