It allows you to :

  • Remain in good standing on French territory for the duration of the validity of your visa.

  • Leave France, beyond the 3 months following your arrival, without needing a visa to return.

You will need:

  • a valid email address,

  • information on your visa,

  • your date of arrival in France,

  • your home address in France,

  • a bank card to pay online the fee for issuing the residence permit.

Within the first three months following your arrival, you must register and validate your visa at the following address: Accueil | Étrangers en France (interieur.gouv.fr)

A. Sign in : administration-etrangers-en-france.interieur.gouv.fr;

B. Inform your VISA’s information : number, start and end date of validity, date of issue and reason for stay

C. Enter the complementary information such as family situation, phone number, email…

D. Pay the tax online, you will need a stamp:

Option 1

You can purchase an electronic stamp online. The option is displayed on the screen.

Option 1

You enter the number of the electronic stamp you previously purchased.

E. Download the confirmation of the validation of your VLS-TS. That’s it, your visa has been validated !
