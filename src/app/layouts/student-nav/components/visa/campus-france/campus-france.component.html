How to pay?

After submitting you will receive a link, then you will have to pay fees.

There are different types of payment

(depending on your country)

  • Pay online.

  • By check or credit card (NO CASH) directly to your Campus France.

Only paid and submitted applications will be processed.

In most countries, the answer will come after 1 - 2 weeks

For Tunisia

Students must wait for their file to be accepted first before the payment.

Once Campus France validates your file, they will reach out to schedule a Campus France interview.

In case any errors are identified, they will request that you make the necessary modifications before resubmitting your file

After checking your file, Campus France sends

1st message

Informing you that your application has been validated

2nd message

Asking you to make your online appointment for the Campus France interview
