TLS/VFS

WHAT IS VFS OR TLS ?

  • Once you have completed the France Visa application, the next step is to schedule an appointment through TLS - VFS.

  • TLS - VFS operates on behalf of the French embassy to maintain the quality of the visa process and provide assistance to students in obtaining their visas. They facilitate the appointment booking process and support students throughout the visa application journey.

COLLECT PASSPORT ?

  • You will receive a visa that will be stamped to your passport after your
    application has been reviewed

  • Your passport can be mailed to your home or the nearest VFS or TLS center
    within 2-3 weeks.

  • You will be informed of each step by VFS through text messages.
