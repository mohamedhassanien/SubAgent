Bank Account

Opening a bank account is an easy yet very important process.

  • You first need to pick a bank that has a plan which suits you.

  • You then proceed to get an appointment to present them your documents and sign the contract.

  • You usually receive your card within 2 weeks, but it varies from one bank to the other.

Required documents

Identification
Proof of residence
Attestation of enrolment
or a student card.

Things to take into account

  • Currency exchange rates

  • Transaction fees

  • International payments/transactions

  • Partnerships with your University

  • Partnerships with your domestic bank

  • Student accounts

  • English speaking customer service
