La carte Vitale

Why?

For partial reimbursement of your health expense (visit to the doctor, medical check-ups, etc.).

When?

As soon as possible after your
academic registration in your higher education institution.

How?

Go to https://etudiant-etranger.ameli.fr
(available in French, English and Spanish).

How much?

It’s free of charge. French and international
students don’t pay to register to French
social security.

  • Start your procedures on the website of the health insurance dedicated to foreign students as soon as you are registered in your establishment.

  • If you came with a VLS-TS, you complete your file as soon as you have validated your residence permit and opened a bank account in France .

  • If you came with a VLS-TS, you created your personal account on ameli.fr .
