What is les Crous?

Le crous is a regional organisation providing student bursaries, university halls of residence, reception of foreign students, student cultural activities, and student restaurants.

Their main missions are

Allocating and maintaining accommodation for students, taking social criteria into account.

Managing individual financial assistance
(grants, emergency aids…).

Supporting students with the
help of social workers.

Informing students in their everyday life.

Organizing cultural
activities.

If you’re a student, you are eligible to benefit from their two most important services which are accommodation and Crous restaurants.

Residence CROUS

Seeing that Crous student residences are the least expensive option in terms of accommodation, the demand is very high, and you are not always guaranteed a spot after submitting your application. It is highly recommended that you have a plan B.

Restaurant Universitaire:

Crous restaurants can usually be found on the University campus and can you get meals for as cheap as 3€, all weekdays from 11:30am to 2:00pm. You can pay using your Student Izly card after creating an account on Izly.com.
