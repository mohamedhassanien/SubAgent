Our Story

Salah and Nicolas met while studying business in La Rochelle, France. Prior to that, they both lived abroad, Salah in Egypt and Nicolas in Qatar. They quickly understood the difficulty that international students face when they want to study abroad. At the end of their studies, they decided to join forces in order to solve this problem and to support future students. The story begins in 2019 in Paris where they decided, like a navigator looking for his promised land, to create StudentGator in order to allow students to reach their dream school.
