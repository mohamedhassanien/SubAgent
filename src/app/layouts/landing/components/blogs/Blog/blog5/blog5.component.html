StudentGator Process

22 August, 2022

////////////////////////////////

Deciding to Study Abroad is a fascinating decision followed by a million dreams, aspirations, a high salary and a hope of a good life. The idea itself is rejoiceful until the strike of reality! First of all, foreign education comes with a lot of investment. It is an investment of time as well as money. Students usually think that the Return on Investment post their education would be really high and would cover all their expenses. Well, that’s completely possible and true to every extent but not every scenario is as rosy as it seems especially when the education sector is booming as a business

Filtering out the right information from the internet is a difficult task and the over-reliance on agencies has tremendously increased the risk of students making the wrong decision. A wrong decision can cause a student plenty of hardship on a foreign land and may cost his/her future. This is where StudentGator comes to the rescue, StudentGator as a team consists of students who already studied or are currently studying in France. We know the exact situation of the country since we are based in France and can guide students seamlessly through each and every process.

1. Profile Screening

Before suggesting any school it is necessary to understand the complete profile of the student. Understanding the profile is important in determining a course which will suit the student’s interest and background.

2. Identifying the University and Programs -

Post screening and identifying the student’s interest, we would identify the best suiting program and school where our students must apply. Students are provided with multiple options to choose from, we advise students to read the course structure carefully before applying to any school/university. Post identification, the student selects a course of his/her choice

3. Preparation of documents

The document requirement for application to a French university/school is normally the same but few universities have a slightly different application procedure and require some additional documentation. StudentGator thoroughly assists its students in preparing all the necessary documents. Main document of focus is the motivation letter or the motivation questionnaire which are asked during the application process. These questions must be well drafted and curated showing clear motivation and interest. We’ll thoroughly help you in drafting a great motivation letter

4. Interview preparation

Once the application is selected, students will have to take an interview with the school. Since we have good connections with the school and plenty of our students have been through the interview process, we can thoroughly guide you with the expected questions and if necessary will take your mock interview to boost your confidence.

5. Selection & Registration

Voila, Congratulations! Since you have applied through StudentGator, you are ought to be selected to your dream university/school. Post your selection to the university/school, we will guide you through the registration with the school. Generally students have to pay a deposit amount to the school to secure your seat.

Post Selection :

1. Campus France

This is a simple yet a slight lengthy process. We assist you thoroughly in the application process on the Campus France portal.

2. Accommodation

ince we are based in France, we certainly have a good network and our students are also ready to help to find accommodation. Thus, we can help you put in contact with the right person. Also, while finding accommodation students can encounter a lot of scams, we help students to identify if their accommodation proposal is legit or not.

www.messervices.etudiant.gouv.fr/envole/

3. Visa Application

It is the most crucial stage for every student who wishes to study abroad. Since we went through this process and we have students who have gone through the same process, we will be your best source of guidance throughout the application process. We have a dedicated blog on step by step visa process which you must take a look at.

4. Guidance on Arrival

We are proud to say that we are not consultancies but we are student’s best friends. We aim to help students throughout their journey in France. Post-arrival students may face problems in OFII, CAF, Assurance Maladie etc, we are always here to help to the maximum of our capacity.

5. StudentGator Community

We all know, networking is always an important aspect when it comes to creating opportunities and also making friends. We organise monthly meet-ups at our office or at our campus tours so that all our international students can interact and know each other

StudentGator Team, always strives to make student’s lives easy and help them fly through the process easily. We don’t charge any money from our students, all our services are free of cost. To keep yourself updated with all the latest information about France and with necessary student updates, follow us on instagram @studentgator
