What Is The Deposit And How To Pay It ?

Definition:

A deposit is a partial payment of the total amount due to the School

Amount:

The deposit is required by all schools after receivingan acceptance
the amount varies by school. It is generally between 2000 and 5000 euros.

Usefulness:

Deposits are requested by the schools in order to manage the number of students
admitted and to anticipate the number of seats available. This also allows them
to respond quickly to students on the waiting list.
Another reason is to prevent students from booking seats when they plan to change schools.
At the expense of other students who had the school as their first choice.
After receiving the deposit, the school will send you the final letter of acceptance to start the VISA procedures.

When to pay it ?

In general, the deposit must be paid 3 weeks after receiving admission to the school. After this date, your place is no longer guaranteed.

How to pay it ?

  • By credit card: Some schools accept credit card payments directly on their website
  • By Bank Transfer: The RIB ('statement of banking identity) of the schools can be
  • requested to pay, be sure to write your name in the reason of the transfer
  • FLYWIRE: In some countries, it is difficult to make international paiments. Some schools, therefore, offer payment by flywire to facilitate this. (See list of schools pay.flywire.com )

Can I get a refund?

Before paying the deposit it is always important to read the school's general conditions. However, here are the conditions that we find for the Grandes Ecoles:
  • Applicant's Right to Cancel: Applicants may request a refund of the tuition deposit within the 14-day cooling-off period without giving a reason.
  • In case of visa refusal, the school will accept the refund. Of course, you will have to show proof of the refusal
  • If the student has not passed the year or failed to provide a language test and therefore does not have the required admission criteria to pursue his studies.
  • In the event of serious unforeseen circumstances (death, accident, or serious illness), schools may review the situation and grant a refund
Caution! : In the event of visa denial for fraudulent reasons, some schools may refuse a refund.

What Is The Deposit And How To Pay It ?

Definition:

A deposit is a partial payment of the total amount due to the School

Amount:

The deposit is required by all schools after receiving an acceptance, the amount varies by school. It is generally between 2000 and 5000 euros.

Usefulness:

This also allows them to respond quickly to students on the waiting list. Another reason is to prevent students from booking seats when they plan to change schools. At the expense of other students who had the school as their first choice. After receiving the deposit, the school will send you the final letter of acceptance to start the VISA procedures.

In general, the deposit must be paid 3 weeks after receiving admission to the school. After this date, your place is no longer guaranteed.
